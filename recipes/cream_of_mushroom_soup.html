Cream of Mushroom Soup

Description

A warm bowl of soup so deliciously creamy with tender bites of mushroom pieces.
Full flavoured with garlic, onions and herbs subtle enough to shine through and
compliment the natural flavour of mushrooms without overpowering it.
Cooked with easy to find ingredients you may already have in your kitchen,
you are minutes away from a satisfying bowl of creamy goodness!
