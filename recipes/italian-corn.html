Italian Corn

Description

This is a delicious seasoned corn side dish. DELICIOUS. Must TRY NOW!!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 1/2 cups whole kernel sweet corn
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet over high heat.
  2. Heat butter in hot skillet until almost completely metlted.
  3. Stir in corn.
  4. Season cron with Italian seasoning, salt, and black pepper.
  5. Cook seasoned corn until light golden brown, about 5 minutes.
